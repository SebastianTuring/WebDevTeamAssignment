Event Info:

Our Theme Park holds different events throurough the year. Our main seasonal events are Christmas and Hallowen but we also hold other events daily

Daily Events

Races:

This event is open for everyone older than 12 years old, those who want to participate go to the race track and the staff will provide further information. The winners get a "One Week Ticket" as a reward

Daily Event Times and Dates:

Tuesdays, Thursdays and Satudays, the time depends on the staff decision.

Talent Show:

This event is open for everyone older than 5 years old, those who want to participate go to the platform in the main square and the staff will provide further information. The winner gets a "One Week Ticket" as a reward

Daily Event Times and Dates:

Tuesdays, Thursdays and Satudays, the time depends on the staff decision.

Special Events

Halloween:

This event is open to everyone, it's Halloween! Visitors are encouraged to come to the park with their favourite costumes and around 10:00 pm a contest will be held on October 31st so every person can vote for the costume they like the best. The prize for the winner is a coupon of 90% off on up to 20 different candies from our Candy Shop

Daily Event Times and Dates:

From October 5 to October 31, 12:00pm to 1:00am

Christmas:

This event is open to everyone, Merry Christmas! We want everyone to have a great time so come with warm clothing so that the low temperatures won't get in the way of your fun. On December 24th at midnight, we will put the star on the big Christmas tree at the centre of the park, be sure not to miss it!

Daily Event Times and Dates:

From december 20 to january first, 4:00pm to 1:00am
